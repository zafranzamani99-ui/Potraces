FAQ

Quick answers.

When does Potraces launch?
It's in beta now. Join the waitlist and we'll message you once, when it's live.
What if I earn from a few different places?
That's exactly the point. Kuih orders, gig shifts, freelance jobs — log them all and see what came in, in one calm view.
Can I use it for Raya season then go back to personal?
Yes. Switch between personal and seller mode anytime — your stuff stays put in each space.
Is my data safe?
It stays on your phone by default, and only on your own cloud if you turn on backup. We don't sell it.
Does it work offline?
Yep. Log even with patchy signal — it saves on your phone and syncs later.
Is it free?
Free during the beta, no card needed. Fair pricing comes later and we'll tell you first.
Is this financial advice?
No. Potraces shows your own money clearly so you can decide — it's a tracker, not an advisor.

## [1] critical/bug — src/store/debtStore.ts:195
**deletePayment refunds wallet inside the store while every caller also reverses it — wallet ends +amount on every wallet-linked payment deletion**

debtStore.deletePayment (lines 195-197) unconditionally calls addToWallet(payment.walletId, payment.amount) after removing the payment. Result in all four cases: wallet balance ends exactly payment.amount higher than correct.

_Fix:_ Remove the addToWallet block (lines 165-166 deletedPayment capture and 195-197) from debtStore.deletePayment; callers own wallet sync. Alternatively make the store the single owner (direction-aware, mode-aware) and strip all caller-side reversals — but the caller-owned pattern already matches the documented sync rule, so deleting the store refund is the surgical fix.

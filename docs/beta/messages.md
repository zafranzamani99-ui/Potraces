# Potraces Beta — WhatsApp Messages

Ready to paste. EN first, then casual gen-z BM. The data-loss warning + "keep your own records in
parallel" appears in the kickoff (and in the agreement clause 6). Send the kickoff **only after** the
tester has signed the agreement and you've sent the install link.

## Kickoff (Day 0)

### EN

```
Hey! Thanks for jumping in to test *Potraces* — a money app I'm building for people whose income isn't a fixed salary (sellers, gig folks, savers). 3 testers only, you're one of them. RM50 for you at the end. Here's everything:

*1) Install (Android 8+ only)*
I'll send you a link/QR. Tap it, download the APK, allow 'install from unknown sources' if asked, open it. It's not on Play Store yet — totally normal for a beta. On first open you'll see a quick beta notice — read it, then tap to continue.

*2) Please read this (important, not boilerplate)*
Potraces is PRE-RELEASE. Bugs are expected — that's literally why you're here. Cloud sync is switched OFF for this test, so your data lives on your phone only. PLEASE keep your usual money records (notes/another app) running in parallel this week — do NOT rely on Potraces as your only record yet. There's a built-in *Backup & Restore* in Settings — please run a backup once you've added a few things. Use realistic amounts, not your actual bank balances.

*3) What I need from you*
Use it like it's your own money tracker. When something breaks, confuses you, or feels off — screenshot it and drop it in this group with: what you tapped, what you expected, what happened. Also screenshot the version line at the bottom of Settings so I know which build it is. Small annoyances count too.

*4) The 7-day plan (light, ~5 min/day)*
Day 0: install + add your first wallet & log 1 income + 1 expense.
Day 1-2: scan a receipt, try the Notes / AI quick-add.
Day 3: I'll check in — quick chat.
Day 4-5: set a budget OR a savings goal, try a debt/split with a friend's name.
Day 6: run a Backup, poke anything you haven't touched.
Day 7: I send a short wrap form, you reply, RM50 goes out.

*5) RM50 payout*
After you finish Day 7 + send feedback, I DuitNow / bank transfer / TNG you RM50 within 48 hours. Send me your payout number whenever.

Any Q, just ask here. Jom!
```

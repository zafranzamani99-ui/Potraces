- Actually use the app during the week — add some wallets, transactions, and try the main features.
- Send us your feedback (bugs you hit, your phone model) through our WhatsApp group **or** the in-app

We'll pay you RM50 (Ringgit Malaysia fifty) once you've met the completion bar below, paid by **DuitNow,

You earn the RM50 when ALL of these are done within the test week:
  (a) you installed the app and used it on at least **3 separate days**;
  (b) you logged at least **10** real or realistic entries (e.g. wallets/transactions/debts);
  (c) you submitted at least one written feedback report — **via WhatsApp or the in-app feedback option** —
      listing at least **3 specific observations** (bugs, confusions, or suggestions), each with the
      screen name and what you expected.
Partial or no completion = no payment, but no hard feelings. We may, at our discretion, still pay if you
got blocked by a bug that was our fault.

The app and anything you see in it (features, screens, designs) are confidential and pre-release. While
the beta is running and until we publicly launch:
- don't post screenshots or videos of the app publicly (social media, groups, etc.);
- don't share or forward the APK / install link to anyone else;
- don't write public reviews or describe unreleased features.
You can of course talk to us directly about anything.

**5. Feedback & ideas**
Any feedback, bug reports, or ideas you give us, we can use freely — to fix, improve, or build features —
with no payment beyond the RM50 and no obligation to use it or credit you. You're not giving up anything
you own outside this feedback.

**6. Beta data warning (please read)**
This is unfinished software. It can have bugs, and data you enter could be lost, corrupted, or wiped
during an update. Cloud sync of personal data is currently turned OFF; your data lives mainly on your
phone. The app has a "Backups & Restore" feature in Settings — please use it.
Because of this:
- **DO NOT use Potraces as your only record of your money during the beta. Keep your normal records
  (notebook, spreadsheet, other app) in parallel.**
- DO NOT enter anything you can't afford to lose or wouldn't want exposed if something breaks. Use
  realistic amounts rather than your true bank balances.
By signing, you confirm you understand and accept this risk. We're not liable for any loss of data or
money caused by using this pre-release app, beyond the RM50.

**7. Your personal data (PDPA)**
To run this beta and pay you, we collect: your name and phone number (to pay the RM50), your phone model
and Android version (to debug), and the feedback you send. We use it only to run the test, fix the app,
and pay you. We keep it only as long as needed for the beta and payment records, then delete it (target:
**within 90 days after the beta ends**). You can ask us to show or delete your data anytime by contacting us.
Your data is stored on Supabase (servers in Singapore) and may be handled by the tools the app uses:
Anthropic and Google Gemini (AI, US), Telegram (verification), Stripe (payments), Vercel and Expo
(hosting/updates). By signing, you consent to this.

**8. The basics**
- This is voluntary and independent — not employment.
- This agreement is governed by the laws of Malaysia.

- This is the whole deal between us for this beta.

## Lawyer-flag points (review before scaling beyond this 3-person beta)

- **Liability cap vs PDPA duty.** Clause 6 caps our liability for data/money loss at RM50, but PDPA 2010
  (as amended 2024) imposes a non-waivable Security Principle duty on us as data user, plus mandatory
  breach-notification and (from 2025) Data Protection Officer obligations. A consent + RM50 cap does NOT
  contract out of statutory PDPA liability or the breach-notification duty. Fine at 3-tester hobby scale;
  get a lawyer to confirm the disclaimer wording and PDPA breach/DPO posture BEFORE scaling or charging users.
- **Cross-border transfer.** Tester data flows to Supabase (Singapore) and US processors (Anthropic,
  Google Gemini). PDPA 2024 tightened cross-border rules (adequacy/whitelist). Consent here covers the
  beta; before public launch a lawyer should confirm the transfer basis and whether a written
  processor/DPA agreement is needed with each foreign processor.
- **AI processing of financial data.** Feedback or app data routed to Anthropic/Gemini may contain
  personal financial details. For a paid public product (not this beta) a lawyer should review whether
  that triggers additional sensitive-data handling/disclosure obligations under PDPA, and whether to
  anonymise before sending to AI. (Mitigation this round: testers are steered to realistic-not-real amounts.)

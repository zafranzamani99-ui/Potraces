# Potraces — Legal & Compliance Handling Plan (v1.0.0)

> **Not legal advice.** Engineering-led reconciliation of a four-lens deliberation (PDPA/fintech counsel, fintech regulatory containment, pragmatic solo-founder/COO, in-repo engineer/red-teamer). It tells you what to fix, in what order, and what truly needs a paid lawyer. Get a written counsel opinion before you (a) ship a personal sign-in, (b) wire a payment provider, or (c) start charging. Verified against the repo on 2026-06-11.

---

## Summary (read this first)

Potraces is **more defensible than a typical Malaysian fintech at this stage** because of three off-switches that are real in code:
- **Personal cloud sync is OFF** — `settingsStore` forces `personalSyncEnabled=false` at init, on reset, and on rehydrate; there is no UI to enable it, and there is no personal sign-in. The contact-PII cloud path is **dead code today**.
- **Payments never touch funds** — `qrProvider.ts` defaults to `'none'`; both PSP stubs throw. Phase-1 DuitNow QR is the seller's own standee (bank-to-bank). Potraces is never in the money path.
- **The privacy notice is already bilingual EN+BM** and pins Supabase to Singapore (ap-southeast-1, a PDPA-grade jurisdiction).

So the launch work is **copy + a handful of mapper/prompt lines + one asset swap + one HTML file** — not re-architecture, not a lawyer retainer. The genuinely dangerous, genuinely cheap items are smaller than the risk register's headline ("cross-border = Critical"): the most weaponisable thing in the whole repo is **one false sentence in `privacy.html`** ("we do not read your contacts"), which the app's own code disproves.

**Six v1.0.0 blockers, all small:**
1. Fix the false "we do not read your contacts" line (EN + BM) + add missing processors + remove unused `WRITE_CONTACTS`.
2. Curtail third-party PII to the AI — **TWO** US LLMs receive it (Anthropic via `aiService.ts` **and** Gemini), not Gemini-only [critic correction]. Free-prose notes have no reliable name tokens to mask, so prefer **signing Anthropic + Google zero-retention/no-training DPAs + disclosure + AI opt-in** over a brittle masker; scrub receipt card numbers at OCR output before the LLM call. Fires with sync OFF.
3. Add a bilingual "not financial advice" disclaimer + AI system-prompt guardrail (zero such text exists today).
4. Swap the shipped logo PNGs — 8 e-wallet **+ 21 bank logos in `assets/bank-logo/` (require()d in `premium.ts`)** [critic correction] — for the generic icon; repoint refs and pass a build BEFORE deleting any PNG (deleting first white-screens the app).
5. Surface the corrected notice at first run with one affirmative tap (turns a good notice into real consent).
6. Add a local-`bak:*` purge to account deletion so the deletion right is actually complete.

**One hard gate (NOT a launch blocker):** rewrite the sync mappers to stop uploading third-party `phone`/`email` **before personal sign-in ever ships**. Fix it now while the path is dormant and the fix is free.

**Defer behind real triggers:** TIA/DPAs/SCCs (sign-in/scale), DPO (~10k financial-data users), SST (before monetising — pick IAP-vs-direct now), PSP activation (BNM-licensed PSP, written "technical referrer" confirmation). The CPETTR order-page upgrade is "soon", gated on **promoting** the storefront, not on the binary shipping.

## Decisions only the founder can make

1. **Contact-sync shape (gate on sign-in):** display-name + salted-phone-hash merge key (recommended) vs on-device `contactId` only. Decide now, while the data path is dormant and the fix is free — deciding after data exists means a backfill + remote wipe.
2. **Billing channel:** IAP (marketplace-facilitator remits SST) vs direct Stripe (you register/charge). Lock before building any paywall. Recommended: IAP.
3. **Public product framing:** commit to "money tracker — doesn't hold or move money" in store listing + onboarding (keep internal `Wallet` type).
4. **Whether/when to flip the off-switches:** treat each of `personalSyncEnabled`, a personal sign-in screen, and `qrProvider != 'none'` as a deliberate, precondition-gated event recorded in `FINAL_BEFORE_DEPLOY`.
5. **CCC/BNPL scope line:** confirm the no-credit, no-collection, no-counselling posture as a permanent product guardrail.

## What truly needs a paid lawyer (and not before its trigger)

1. **PSP activation (before flipping `qrProvider`):** written confirmation from a BNM-licensed PSP (Fiuu/HitPay) that Potraces is a **technical referrer, not an acquirer/EMI**, and funds never land with Potraces. (FSA — operating a payment system / issuing e-money without approval is a serious offence.)
2. **Platform-vs-seller T&Cs for the order page (Reg 7, CPETTR 2024):** the one paragraph pushing per-product disclosure duty onto sellers, plus a buyer-facing privacy line. (The HTML disclosure edits in #10 are DIY; this T&C split is the lawyer bit.)
3. **PDPA review before sign-in/scale:** sign-off on the per-destination TIAs and the signed DPAs/SCCs, and on the DPO appointment/registration near ~10k financial-data subjects.
4. **SST registration** once revenue approaches the ~RM500k threshold (accountant + lawyer), if selling direct rather than via IAP.

Everything else in this plan is DIY copy, config, small code, or documentation. **No retainer is needed to ship v1.0.0.**
